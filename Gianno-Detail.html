Product Details

Designed in partnership with the Tyler, the Creator, the Golf Wang x Converse Gianno Low ‘Black’ delivers a neutral colorway of a chunky silhouette that takes its cues from ‘90s sneakers. The layered upper is constructed from black suede, accented with reflective detailing on the eyestay and lace loops, as well as the white netting that covers the collar and tongue. A vintage off-white finish coats the sculpted EVA midsole, supported underfoot by a high-traction rubber outsole.
